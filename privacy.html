Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

Mileage Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or location data to any servers.

Data Usage

  • GPS Location: Used solely to calculate mileage at the time of your request (Start/End Trip). Location data is never stored remotely.
  • Trip History: Stored locally in your device’s browser using localStorage. You control your own data.
  • Network Activity: The app may use internet connectivity to fetch route distance via Google Maps Directions API. No identifying data is shared during this request.

Data Sharing

This app does not share your data with third parties. We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or ads.
